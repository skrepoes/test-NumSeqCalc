Instructions

Enter a positive number into the calculator field and press Calculate to retrieve the numeric sequences.

The following sequences will be calculated returned:

Numeric Sequence
All numbers up to and including your number.
Odd Number Sequence
All odd numbers up to and including your number.
Even Number Sequence
All even numbers up to and including your number.
Multiples Substitution Sequence
All numbers up to and including your number. With the following substitutions:
  • C if your number is a multiple of 3.
  • E if your number is a multiple of 5.
  • Z if your number is a multiple of both 3 and 5.
Fibonacci Number Sequence
All fibonacci numbers up to and including your number.
